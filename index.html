Subarnaduti Paul

Ph.D. Researcher · TU Darmstadt & University of Bremen

I am a Ph.D. candidate specializing in continual learning algorithms, with expertise in computer vision, multimodal AI, and Bayesian uncertainty modeling. My research drives the sequential adaptation of foundation models through advances in continual learning and machine unlearning, with a strong focus on scalability and computational efficiency.

I am passionate about advancing interpretability and fairness in large-scale models, and eager to contribute to both academic and applied real-world applications. I am currently based in Bremen, Germany.
