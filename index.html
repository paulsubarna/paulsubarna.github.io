I work on the sequential adaptation of foundation models — how large models can keep learning new tasks, forget what they must, and stay honest about what they don't know. My research advances continual learning and machine unlearning algorithms with a strong focus on scalability and computational efficiency, and I care deeply about interpretability and fairness in large-scale models.
